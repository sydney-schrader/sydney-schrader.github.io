our vision

We are committed to introducing adolescents to Jesus Christ and helping them grown in their faith. Young Life also aims to establish and maintain a presence at all high schools and middle schools in the local area. New clubs are started annually and the influence of Young Life is ever growing in the community of Austin, Texas.
